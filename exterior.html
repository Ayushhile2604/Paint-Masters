Frequently Asked Questions

How long does exterior paint typically last?

Exterior paint typically lasts 5–10 years depending on weather, surface type, and paint quality.

Do you offer waterproofing solutions?

Absolutely. We offer waterproof primers and elastomeric coatings designed for high-moisture zones.

What surfaces can you paint?

We paint concrete, wood, metal, stucco, and brick – each with appropriate surface preparation.
